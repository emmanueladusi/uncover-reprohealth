You're not alone

If you're scared, start here.

Someone who's just started experiencing severe symptoms often doesn't need more facts first — they need to know they're not alone.

💌

"What I wish someone told me at 13"

It's not "just cramps" if it's stopping your life. You're not dramatic for asking questions. Keep asking until someone actually listens.

🧭

What to do if you're scared

Tell one person — a school nurse, counselor, parent, or friend. You don't need the full picture figured out to start; you just need to say "something feels wrong."

📣

How to ask for help

"I've been in pain and I don't think it's normal" is a complete sentence. You don't owe anyone a perfect explanation to deserve to be taken seriously.
