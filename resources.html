School & community support.

You don't have to navigate this by yourself — these are people and services built to help.

🏫

Your school nurse

Usually the fastest, most private person to talk to about symptoms during the school day. No appointment needed.

💬

Your school counselor

For the emotional side of things — pain, stress, feeling dismissed, or just needing to talk it through.

03

When to seek help — and how urgently.

📅

Book a regular appointment if...

Symptoms are uncomfortable but manageable — irregular cycles, ongoing cramps that respond to pain relief, or new skin/hair changes you're curious about.

⏱

Seek care soon if...

Pain is disrupting school or sleep, bleeding is unusually heavy or prolonged, or symptoms are getting worse over a few weeks.

Emergency

If pain is sudden, severe, and unlike anything before — or you're fainting, vomiting, or bleeding through more than a pad an hour — treat it as an emergency.
